# HybridGenesis-EvolutionGame

## Vision

HybridGenesis-EvolutionGame is an innovative evolution simulator where you observe and influence the development of hybrid alien species in a living sandbox world. Watch as unique organism traits, behaviors, and cultures emerge over time through mutation, genetic fusion, and environmental adaptation.

## Features

- **Real-time Observable Evolution**: Witness species evolve before your eyes
- **Interactive Genetics**: Influence genetic traits and mutations
- **History Tracking**: Document the evolutionary journey of your species
- **Dynamic Biomes**: Explore diverse environments that shape adaptation
- **Scientific Tools**: Analyze data and track evolutionary patterns
- **Shareable Evolution Highlight Reels**: Capture and share your most exciting evolutionary moments
